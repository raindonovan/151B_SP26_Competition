## §11 Build-brainstorm magic (raw phrasings & offhand ideas — to mine later)
*Texture, not just tangibles. Rough by design. Tight actionable version: see ROADMAP_artifacts_and_projects.md.*
- The split that clicked: **artifacts (what you write) vs projects (what you build).**
- "We're gonna do all of them" — committed to all four builds, not picking one.
- **Build > compete.** Ranked competitions = "beat hundreds of people," which we don't want. Hackathons are the sweet spot: short (a weekend / one week / two weekends), quality-judged, networky, recruiting-adjacent. "Maybe it's not about wins — something small we do really well and get what we want."
- The sleeper asset: **agentic/multi-agent orchestration** — most market-hot, most differentiating; undervalued in the moment.
- The workflow was a **"butcher shop"** — rough, manual, friction (agents couldn't read/write paths, coordination overhead). → Build the harness we *wished* we'd had. **Pain → product.** "Ran a competition on a hacked-together version, then built the thing that fixes it."
- **Domain-crossed build = the real moat.** Pilot / EV-fleet founder / 2M-sub-channel strategist × agentic skill = a defensible, story-rich product a generic ML eng can't build as well. Fun *because* you care about the domain.
- Research-diligence agent: deliciously meta — "an agent that tells you if your idea's already been done."
- The visualizer = **"EIB made tangible."** A living, clickable pipeline beats a static repo. Triple duty: eye-candy + teaching + proof-of-understanding.
- **Replay-first, not live.** A flawless replay of *real* data beats a fragile live GPU run that dies in an interview.
- Quote bank for the blog (recurring meta-lessons): "the contribution is the autopsy, not the skeleton"; "you're not lost anymore, you're circling a runway you're already cleared to land on"; "stop frame-shopping and commit to motion"; "every frame we reach for is pre-empted as a concept — novelty lives in the empirics"; "we're prime to win something."
- Career frame: job / internship / residency / new-grad — same lever (the portfolio); **build is the lever that opens them; ship + apply in parallel.**
- Live open threads: rare-trace experiment (GO; diligence owed); timeline + priority (next); what to enter after the 10 weeks.
