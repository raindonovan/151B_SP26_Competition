\[
x = 2by^{2}\quad\Longrightarrow\quad y^{2}= \frac{1}{2b}\,x .

\[
y^{2}=4ax,
\]

where \(a\) is the focal length (distance from vertex to focus).

\[
4a = \frac{1}{2b}\;\Longrightarrow\; a = \frac{1}{8b}.
\]

Hence the focal length of the reflecting surface is \(\displaystyle \frac{1}{8b}\).
