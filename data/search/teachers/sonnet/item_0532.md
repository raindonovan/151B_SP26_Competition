## Reasoning + Response
For a conic with focus at the origin, eccentricity e, and vertical directrix x = -d (where d > 0), the polar form is:

$$r = \frac{ed}{1 - e\cos\theta}$$

$$ed = \frac{7}{2} \cdot \frac{1}{4} = \frac{7}{8}$$

$$r = \frac{7/8}{1 - \frac{7}{2}\cos\theta}$$
