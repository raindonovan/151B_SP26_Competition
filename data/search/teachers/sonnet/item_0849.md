## Reasoning + Response
We need to compute $\sum_{k=1}^{n} 8\left(\frac{k}{n}\right)$.

$$\sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{8k}{n} = \frac{8}{n} \sum_{k=1}^{n} k = \frac{8}{n} \cdot \frac{n(n+1)}{2} = 4(n+1)$$
