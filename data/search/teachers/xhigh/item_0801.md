The condition only restricts the map on \(A\). The affine space decomposes as
\[
\operatorname{Hom}(V,W)\cong \operatorname{Hom}(A,W)\oplus \operatorname{Hom}(V/A,W).
\]
The rank \(\le k\) determinantal variety in \(\operatorname{Hom}(A,W)\) has dimension
\[
k(l+n-k),
\]
and the unrestricted part \(\operatorname{Hom}(V/A,W)\) has dimension
\[
n(m-l).
\]
So
\[
\dim \Psi_k = n(m-l)+k(l+n-k)-1.
\]
This is not exactly among the options. The closest/most likely intended option is E.
